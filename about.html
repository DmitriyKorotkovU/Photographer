Dmitriy Korotkov
started taking pictures since childhood.

When he first went to school, he already had an old film camera.
He taking pictures of everything that caught his eye,
so the films quickly ended.

Dima is not professionally engaged in this activity.
He just likes to capture good moments in photos,
although this rarely happens.

He doesn't have a good camera.
All photos are taken with an inexpensive phone.

"Maybe someday I will get a quality camera,
study the nature of photography, and share beautiful photos.
But for now, this is just a wish for me, not a dream..."

The name of this site is "If I were a photographer".
(The story is based on real events)
